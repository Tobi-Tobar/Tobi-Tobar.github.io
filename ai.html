AI Art


These are artworks made in collaboration of an AI Program. First one was made using SPADE-COCO, where you make a map using a certain brush and the AI draws in what it thinks I am trying to draw.





These were made using a Style Transfer, where I used my own artstyle and used famous sculptures look like drawings. I always wondered what these famous sculptures would look like if they were illustrations instead of sculptures. They came out looking like a mix of a Lofi cover photo mixed with the Vaporwave aesthetics.
