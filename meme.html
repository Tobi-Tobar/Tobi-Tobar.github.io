Meme Project

I decided to make a meme on the most current interest within my generation and those younger than I. Among Us is a small indie game of ten Crewmates that are on a space ship, having to do tasks to make the ship be at top shape. However 1 or 3 of those crewmates are imposters aimed to kill everyone. I decided to make this meme to express how I felt when I first started playing it. I had no idea what I was doing, so I was always sus (Suspicious) of being an Imposter.
